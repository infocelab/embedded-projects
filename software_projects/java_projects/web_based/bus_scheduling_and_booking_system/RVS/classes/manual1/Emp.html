Working on Employees

Click on the Files on the tool bar
All files will be displayed and click on the Employee
The list of all emplyees will be shown

Registration of new Employee
 To Register a new Employee click on the ADD ENTRY button
Registration window will appear

Enter all the required details on the fields
Click on the date buttons to adjust the dates
Click on add to add the bus
Cancel to exit the process
And Clear to start a fresh

Updating the Employee's Details Do not change Employee Number
To update the Employee, Click on the Employee you want to
update on the table
Then click on the update button
A Window for updating the the Employee will appear

During update you can Edit or Delete the Employee

To edit make the new changes and click on update
To Delete click on the Delete button
You can search the Employee to be updated by entering
the Employee number and Clicking on search.
The REFRESH button on the table will update the table
