To perform Castling
1. drag the King to the block containing the Rook (i.e. drag the king over the rook) and release the mouse button i.e. drag and drop the king over the rook.
2. Or drag and drop the rook over the king
3. Either way if Castling is allowed, the king and the rook will perform castling and
 will be placed in the after-castling position. The location where the king and the rook will be placed will be determined by the program depending on the castling rule. The player does not have to specify the after-castling location or drag the king and rook to those after-castling location.
